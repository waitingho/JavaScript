About the illustration

Created by artist: Will Phillips Jr.

In honor of the new HTML5 logo recently released by the W3C, I am proud to present this illustration. Inspired by the logo's design (created by Ocupop), and eager to embrace and support the progress of new web technologies, I was moved to create this artwork. With this piece I wished to carry on the message it conveys as something new and bold, that shows the magnitude of this movement and how it relates to the future of the web.
